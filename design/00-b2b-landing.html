Common questions

How quickly can I go live? +
Template plans go live in 24 hours. Custom builds take 2-4 weeks. The onboarding wizard takes under 45 minutes — branding, services, payments, team, done.
Can I transfer my existing website / domain? +
Yes. Transfer your domain into our platform with zero downtime. We handle DNS migration, SSL, and email setup. Your old URL keeps working throughout.
Do I need to be a Distance-Selling Pharmacy to sell online? +
Yes, for POM products. We verify your DSP registration before enabling online fulfilment. If you're not yet registered, our DSP Licence Wizard (coming soon) guides you through the GPhC process.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your domain and patient data are always yours. One-click CSV/PDF export for everything: patients, bookings, orders, clinical records. No lock-in, no exit fees.
Is it GPhC / MHRA compliant? +
Yes. PGDs are authored by registered clinical professionals. MHRA Internet Pharmacy logo auto-injects on POM pages. Audit trail is immutable. We're designed for GPhC inspections.
I already use Pharmadoctor. Can I switch? +
Yes. Our PGD library covers the same services (and more). We provide a migration guide and can run both platforms in parallel during transition. Most pharmacies switch fully within 2 weeks.
