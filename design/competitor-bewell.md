# Competitor Analysis: BeWell (bewell.uk)

**Date:** 2026-04-27
**Analyst:** Pharmacy One Stop competitive intelligence

---

## Company Overview

BeWell (bewell.uk) positions itself as "Your partner in care" -- a pharmacy technology platform providing a single interoperable system for community pharmacies across the UK. The platform connects back-office pharmacy operations with front-of-house digital patient experiences.

BeWell is developed by BeWell Innovations (also trading as BeWell UK). The patient app is published by Medicinechest Ltd (trading as The Pharmacy Centre).

---

## Core Platform & Features

### 1. Pharmacy Console (Back-Office)
- Centralised dashboard for pharmacy teams
- Permissions-based access to patient data
- View and process acute and repeat prescriptions
- Auto-status updates through the dispensing workflow
- Full transparency on outstanding/overdue prescriptions

### 2. Patient App (iOS + Android)
- NHS Login supported patient authentication
- Repeat prescription ordering and management
- Real-time prescription status tracking (collection readiness)
- Appointment booking (self-service, 24/7)
- In-app communication with pharmacy (2-way messaging)
- Health history, allergies, medications, vaccination records
- Service discovery and booking by location

### 3. Scheduler / Booking System
- Purpose-built scheduling for clinical services
- Multi-location / multi-branch support ("across your organization")
- In-person and virtual appointment delivery
- Automated reminders and confirmations
- Pre-appointment document/questionnaire delivery via in-app comms
- Service creation, promotion, booking, and management in one place

### 4. Omnichannel Communications
- Single communication thread per patient (by date/time stamp)
- Channels: phone, email, SMS, app notifications, 2-way text
- Historical + open communications visibility
- Permissions-based access for staff

### 5. Patient Data / CRM
- Full platform activity history
- Health history, allergies, medications
- Vaccination records
- Integrated patient data across all touchpoints

### 6. Prescription Management
- Integration with Surgery Online Services for repeat prescriptions
- Acute and repeat prescription processing
- Auto-status updates through dispensing workflow
- Full transparency for pharmacy team and patient

## Technology & Integrations

- **PMR Integration:** Integrated with Cegedim Rx Pharmacy Manager via seamless API
  - Automatic status updates to patients about prescription workflow
  - Dispensing workflow integration
- **NHS Login:** Supported for patient authentication
- **Surgery Online Services:** Integration for repeat prescriptions
- **Mobile:** Native apps on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play)
- **API-driven architecture:** Seamless API integrations for patient-facing features

---

## Partnerships & Ecosystem

BeWell has built a notable partner ecosystem:

1. **NPA (National Pharmacy Association):** Long-standing partnership, expanded to provide "solution-based platform and ecosystem approach to pharmacies." Aligns with NPA's commitment to community pharmacy ecosystem.

2. **AAH Pharmaceuticals:** Partnership to boost patient engagement, improve care outcomes, and drive sustainable growth. AAH is one of the UK's largest pharmaceutical wholesalers.

3. **Cegedim Rx:** Technical integration with Pharmacy Manager PMR system.

4. **PGD-based Service Providers:** BeWell integrates PGD providers into the platform ecosystem (specific PGD providers not named publicly).

5. **Marketing Agencies:** Part of the ecosystem to help pharmacies promote services.

6. **Delivery Method Partners:** Integrated delivery partners (specific names not public).

The ecosystem strategy is notable -- BeWell unites marketing agencies, PGD-based service providers, and delivery partners under one umbrella.

## Target Market

- **Primary:** UK community pharmacies (independent and small chains)
- **Distribution:** Via NPA membership base and AAH customer network
- **Focus:** Pharmacies wanting to expand beyond traditional prescription fulfillment into clinical services, patient engagement, and digital transformation
- **Segment:** NHS-focused pharmacies moving into private services

---

## Pricing

- **Model:** Subscription licence fee (per pharmacy, not publicly disclosed)
- **Structure:** Set out in individual Order Forms
- **No public pricing page** -- requires contact/demo for quote
- **Likely tiered** based on features and pharmacy size, though no confirmation

## Services Supported

- Consultations (in-person and virtual)
- Vaccinations (COVID, flu, travel, etc.)
- Private healthcare services
- Repeat prescription management
- Clinical services via PGD integration
- Appointment booking and management

## Strengths

1. **NPA Partnership:** Massive distribution advantage through NPA's membership base of independent pharmacies
2. **AAH Distribution:** Access to AAH's extensive customer network
3. **Cegedim PMR Integration:** Deep integration with one of the UK's leading PMR systems
4. **Ecosystem Approach:** Unique strategy of bundling PGD providers, marketing, and delivery into one platform
5. **Patient App:** Mature mobile app with NHS Login, prescription management, and booking
6. **Omnichannel Comms:** Comprehensive communication stack (SMS, email, app, phone)
7. **Established Brand:** Several years in market with proven partnerships
8. **NHS Integration:** NHS Login support provides trust and ease for patients

---

## Weaknesses

1. **PMR Lock-in:** Primary integration appears to be Cegedim Rx only -- pharmacies on other PMR systems (e.g., Positive Solutions/EMIS) may be excluded or have limited functionality
2. **No Public Pricing:** Lack of transparency suggests potentially high or variable pricing
3. **No Online Ordering / E-Commerce:** Focused on prescription management and bookings, not online pharmacy sales/home delivery of PGD-supplied medications
4. **No Visible IDV (Identity Verification):** No mention of remote identity verification for online services
5. **No Clinical Review Workflow:** No visible prescriber queue or clinical review system for asynchronous PGD consultations
6. **Limited E-Commerce:** Does not appear to support online product selection, questionnaire-driven PGD supply, or checkout flows
7. **Traditional Pharmacy Model:** Primarily digitises existing pharmacy workflows rather than enabling new online-first service models
8. **Partner Dependency:** Heavy reliance on NPA and AAH for distribution; if partnerships change, growth could stall
9. **No Multi-Tenant SaaS:** Appears to be a single-instance platform per pharmacy rather than a true multi-tenant architecture
10. **No Visible Storefront Builder:** Pharmacies cannot create branded online storefronts for their services
11. **Delivery:** Relies on third-party delivery partners rather than owning the dispatch/tracking experience
12. **No Subscription/Repeat Order Automation:** No visible capability for automated repeat medication subscriptions with home delivery

## Strategic Implications for Pharmacy One Stop

### Where BeWell is ahead:
- **Distribution:** NPA + AAH partnerships give them instant access to thousands of pharmacies
- **PMR Integration:** Deep Cegedim Rx integration is a strong moat for dispensing-focused pharmacies
- **NHS Login:** Patient trust factor
- **Omnichannel Comms:** More mature communication stack
- **Patient App:** Native mobile experience

### Where Pharmacy One Stop is ahead:
- **Online Services Model:** Full PGD-driven online order flow (questionnaire > IDV > clinical review > Rx > dispatch) -- BeWell has nothing comparable
- **Multi-Tenant SaaS:** True platform architecture vs single-pharmacy tool
- **Clinical Workflow:** Prescriber queue, SLA timers, clinical review -- purpose-built for PGD supply at scale
- **E-Commerce:** Complete checkout, payment, and order management
- **Dispatch & Tracking:** Owned shipment tracking with timeline events
- **White-Label Storefronts:** Each pharmacy gets a branded digital presence
- **Platform Economics:** Super-admin, tenant management, tier-based pricing -- built for scale

### Opportunities:
1. **NHS Login integration** would close a gap and increase patient trust
2. **Native mobile app** could match BeWell's app experience
3. **Omnichannel comms** (SMS, in-app messaging) would enhance patient engagement
4. **PMR integrations** (Cegedim, EMIS) would help win pharmacies already using those systems
5. **NPA/wholesaler partnerships** could replicate BeWell's distribution advantage

### Key Differentiator:
Pharmacy One Stop is an **online pharmacy services platform** (PGD-driven, e-commerce-enabled, clinically governed) while BeWell is a **patient engagement and communication platform** for traditional pharmacy operations. They serve overlapping but fundamentally different use cases. BeWell digitises the existing pharmacy; Pharmacy One Stop enables new online revenue streams.
